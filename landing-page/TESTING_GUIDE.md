# Marg Logistics – Complete Testing & Evaluation Guide

Welcome to **Marg**! This guide will walk you through exactly how to test the end-to-end flow of our intelligent logistics orchestration platform. 

The system consists of several dedicated portals that communicate with a unified central intelligence backend. To see the full magic of the platform, we recommend opening these portals in separate tabs (or even separate browser windows side-by-side) so you can watch real-time updates!

## 🔗 The Portals
Here are the live links for testing:
1. **Factory Portal:** [https://margfactory.vercel.app](https://margfactory.vercel.app)
2. **Warehouse Portal:** [https://marg-warehouse-theta.vercel.app](https://marg-warehouse-theta.vercel.app)

## 🚀 End-to-End Workflow Walkthrough

### Step 1: The Origin (Factory Portal)
**Where to go:** `https://margfactory.vercel.app`
**Persona:** Factory Operations Manager

1. **Register/Login:** Click **Register** to create a new Factory Profile. Fill in your details (make sure to use a unique email address).
2. **Dashboard:** Once logged in, you will see your factory operations dashboard.
3. **Create a Shipment / Lot:** 
   - Navigate to the **Create Shipment** or **Create Lot** section.
   - Enter the cargo details, destination warehouse, and requested dispatch time.
   - Submit the request. This pushes the new shipment into the unified backend as `DRAFT` or `PENDING`.

### Step 2: The Orchestrator (Admin / Operations Portal)
**Where to go:** `https://marg-driver.vercel.app/admin/dashboard` (or your designated OS portal)
**Persona:** Logistics Dispatcher / Super Admin

1. **Review Pending Requests:** Go to the Requests or Shipments tab to see the shipment generated by the Factory.
2. **Assign Assets:** 
   - Assign a **Vehicle** (Truck) and a **Driver** to this shipment.
   - Update the status from `PENDING` to `ASSIGNED` or `DISPATCHED`.
3. **Live Telemetry:** You can now open the **Live Tracking** or **Transit Dashboard** to monitor this shipment in real-time.

### Step 3: The Execution (Driver Portal)
**Where to go:** `https://marg-driver.vercel.app/driver/login`
**Persona:** Truck Driver

1. **Driver Login:** Log in using the credentials of the driver you assigned in Step 2.
2. **View Assigned Trips:** The driver's dashboard will show the active assigned shipment.
3. **Progress the Lifecycle:**
   - **Start Trip:** Driver marks the trip as started (heading to factory).
   - **Arrived at Origin:** Driver indicates they have reached the Factory.
   - **Loaded & Dispatched:** Driver confirms the cargo is loaded and they are en route to the Warehouse.
   - *(Optional)* Simulate GPS updates: If the app has location tracking, the backend will receive telemetry data during the journey!

### Step 4: The Destination (Warehouse Portal)
**Where to go:** `https://marg-warehouse-theta.vercel.app`
**Persona:** Warehouse Receiving Manager

1. **Warehouse Login:** Register or log in as a Warehouse Manager.
2. **Incoming Shipments Dashboard:** You will see the shipment the Driver is currently transporting, marked as `IN TRANSIT`.
3. **Dock Bay Allocation:** 
   - Before the truck arrives, click on the shipment and assign it to a specific **Dock Bay** (e.g., D-01).
   - The platform uses predictive ETAs to optimize dock scheduling.
4. **Arrival & Unloading:** 
   - When the driver marks "Arrived at Destination", the warehouse portal will update in real-time.
   - The Warehouse Manager verifies the shipment, unloads the cargo, and marks the shipment as `COMPLETED`.

## 🛠️ What to Look For (Evaluation Criteria)
While testing, keep an eye out for these technical highlights:
- **Instant State Synchronization:** When the driver changes a status (e.g., "Dispatched"), notice how the Factory and Warehouse portals instantly reflect this change.
- **Predictive ETA & Dock Allocation:** Notice how the Warehouse manager can pre-allocate docks based on incoming truck data.
- **Centralized Database:** All 3 React/Next.js portals are reading from a single source of truth—our centralized Django + SQLite/Postgres backend (`https://marg-hacathon.onrender.com/api/v1/`).
- **Dynamic JWT Auth:** The system uses Role-Based Access Control (RBAC). A driver cannot access warehouse metrics, and a factory owner cannot access driver management.

## ⚠️ Troubleshooting
- **400 Bad Request on Registration:** This means the email you are using is already in the database. Please try using a slightly different email (e.g., `test2@email.com`).
- **Missing Data:** Ensure you are logged into the correct role (e.g., you won't see Warehouse data if you are logged in as a Factory Manager).
- **Backend Spin-up Time:** Because the backend is hosted on Render, the very first API call might take 30-50 seconds if the server has gone to sleep. Just be patient on the first login!
